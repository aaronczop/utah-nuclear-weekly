About This Digest

What it covers

Every Sunday, Claude searches the live web for the past week of Utah nuclear news and writes a curated briefing — energy projects, waste policy, uranium mining, legislation, and Hill AFB developments.

Why Utah?

Utah is one of the most active states for nuclear energy development — home to SMR projects, proposed waste storage in Millard County, and Governor Cox's Operation Gigawatt initiative.

How it works

The Anthropic API + web search generates the digest. Gmail sends it. GitHub Pages can host a public archive. Total cost: ~$0.15 per week in API usage.

Cost breakdown

Hosting: free (GitHub Pages)
Sending: free (Gmail)
Scheduling: free (Apps Script)
AI generation: ~$8–15/year
